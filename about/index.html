The Blox, Founder Startup Accelerator Web3 startup accelerator. Born out of repeated asks from founder friends reaching out for growth support. Built the infrastructure around it with the help of former Techstars exec Pat Riley, assembled a team of 5, and supported 25+ pre-seed startups that collectively raised $40M+ from investors like Techstars, Animoca Brands, Ubisoft, and Tim Draper.

Augmented Nation, formerly Metacircle, Co-Founder Think Tank A think tank of 100+ corporate members from McKinsey, Disney, and Nike. Co-founded with Philippe Rodriguez. Contributed to shaping France's JONUM regulatory framework for virtual worlds.

Vortex, Co-Founder DeFi Protocol An automated market maker (AMM) on the Tezos blockchain. Hit $100M in peak trading volume. Backed by the Tezos Foundation. The platform was acquired by startup studio Pyratz Labs.

Smartlink, Co-Founder Blockchain / Fintech A non-custodial escrow solution. Raised $3M. Sought to eliminate trust issues in large cross-border transactions using smart contracts. Phased out of the project.

Sourcing Force, Co-Founder B2B SaaS A procurement SaaS solution. Bootstrapped to $20M ARR. $500M in corporate spend processed through the platform. Made the Financial Times FT1000.

BME Care, Co-Founder B2B Marketplace A B2B platform for PPE supply. Took to $7M in revenue. Built a procurement interface in response to the global PPE shortage caused by Covid.

These days, I split my time between Order Effect, a GTM Intelligence company, and Stakk Ventures, occasionally backing founders.
